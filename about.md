I'm Gareth Stokes, co-founder and CTO of [superit.ai](https://superit.ai). I
build developer tools and write about the decisions behind them.

Right now most of that energy goes into [zinc](https://github.com/garethstokes/zinc),
a git-native, Nix-assisted build tool for Haskell with no version solver. This
site is where I write up the design choices, the trade-offs, and the things that
broke along the way.

You can see zinc driving a real, multi-package project in
[forge](https://github.com/garethstokes/forge), a monorepo that unifies crucible,
manifest, and manifest-evals into a single zinc workspace.

Find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/garethstokes).
