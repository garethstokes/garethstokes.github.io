I'm Gareth Stokes, co-founder and CTO of [superit.ai](https://superit.ai), where
I build agentic AI tools. That's where most of my energy goes.

The rest goes into Haskell. I like to research in it and write up what I find, and
this site is where those findings land.

One of those threads is [zinc](https://github.com/garethstokes/zinc), a git-native,
Nix-assisted build tool for Haskell with no version solver. You can see it driving
a real, multi-package project in [forge](https://github.com/garethstokes/forge), a
monorepo that unifies crucible, manifest, and manifest-evals into a single zinc
workspace.

Find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/garethstokes).
