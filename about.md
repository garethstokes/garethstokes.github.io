I'm Gareth Stokes. I build developer tools and write about the decisions behind
them.

Right now most of that energy goes into [zinc](https://github.com/garethstokes/zinc),
a git-native, Nix-assisted build tool for Haskell with no version solver. This
site is where I write up the design choices, the trade-offs, and the things that
broke along the way.

Find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/garethstokes).
