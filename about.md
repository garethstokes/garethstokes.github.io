I'm Gareth Stokes, co-founder and CTO of [superit.ai](https://superit.ai), where
I build agentic AI tools. That's where most of my energy goes.

The rest goes into Haskell. I like to research in it and write up what I find, and
this site is where those findings land.

Most of it happens in [forge](https://github.com/garethstokes/forge), where I
implement ideas from research papers in Haskell; it gathers projects like
crucible, manifest, and manifest-evals into one workspace. The build tool
underneath is [zinc](https://github.com/garethstokes/zinc), a git-native,
Nix-assisted build tool for Haskell with no version solver.

Find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/garethstokes).
